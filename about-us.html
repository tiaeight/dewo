Discover our mission

We are working for the children's growth

DEWO
is the only box for toys of small or medium size
for kids between 6 and 8 years old
that improves kids’ autonomy bringing together duty and fun
by giving children digital rewards through an application

The first phase of our project was based on the user research, which was essential to understand what parents would like to have for their children. Through this analysis we got the knowledge to go on with the concept. According to interviews, observations and studies, parents want their kid to be more autonomous in tasks that concern organization and objects care, because is what they encounter most difficulties. Dewo, thanks to its animal shape, helps kids to become more responsible in that: the action of tidying up is associated with the action of feeding an animal which is in the kids’ care. In this way, he will enjoy something that was considered as annoying and at the same time he will pleasantly learn something that is important for his future and for his growth.
