App's introduction

We want to bring togheter fun and responsability

According to our research we noticed that kids usually consider annoying the activities that don't give them benefits in a short time. So, we want to create a platform to teach something, but also to create entertainment. This app is essential for the parents, in order to activate Dewo, but also for children, because using it they can enjoy the rewards, custom the animal and discover its family.

The animal appearence is very important: we want to make kids more rensposible through the idea of taking care of a pet. Dewo is designed to become an interactive friends for everyone, adults and children. Moreover we designed it in different heights and width, so it's possible to choose the best sizes according to your needs. You can add in the app only the ones you decide to shop, but then there is anyway the possibility to unlock free friends of the same species through the reward system.

Customizable application


Using Dewo's application, you are able to control your pet and decide when it's time to feed him. When you want that your child will tidy up the room, it's enough to click the button and Dewo will be triggered: he will ask for food, his eyes will change expression and only if someone put objects into his mounth he will be happy. But our app has also another function. If the kid succeed in tidying up his objects, he will get rewards that can be used to customize his friend and unlock his family.

Innovative interaction


One of the main aim of Dewo is to become a cute friend for children. In order to achieve this goal, we designed a structure that uses components as leds and speakers to show different expressions and sounds, like a real pet. This interaction starts when another important component, which is the load cell on the base, notice a change in weight or pressure. All of them are connected to Arduino1 board.
