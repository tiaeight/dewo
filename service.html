Simple solutions for boring activities

The quality of the interaction is one of the aspect that we considered most during the design of Dewo. Each components of the product was studied very carefully, in order to improve the user experience of the customers. Here, we want to highlight three important parts, which are the core of Dewo.

Wireless connection - +

It's essential that you can activate Dewo when you are far from it. Moreover, thanks to this, you can also carry the animal in a digital environment and really engage the child with his reward.

Led and speaker system - +

Dewo is an animal friend and to come alive we implemented a structure able to reproduce expressions and sounds according to the right stimuli.

Load cell - +

This is the technical name of the weight sensor and it's what activate all the interactive parts of Dewo. The animal communicates with the child and it happens when it detect a change.

Connect Dewo


You have to connect Dewo using the Wi-Fi module only the first time in which you use it. Then, for the next uses, it's enough to turn on it on your smartphone, click on the right animal through the app and the whole system will be automatically paired. The product is ready to be used.

Send an order


If you are tired to see objects and toys spread all over the room, it's definitely time to send an order. Launch the app, click on the parents' button and activate Dewo, which will ask for some food. You can also activate notifications in order to to know when someone put an object into the animal.

Send an order


In this step your direct intervention is not required, because in this phase you have just to wait while your kid is tidying up all the stuff. Moreover, you don't have to yell to obligate him: Dewo reacts with eye's movements and sounds when he is hungry and also when an object in put into his belly.

Send an order


When your child says you that he accomplished the task, you can control if the tidy satisfies you or if there are other objects to fix. In the first case, click the YES button and he will get a reward on the app. On the contrary, press the other button to trigger Dewo again and come back to the previous point.

Get reward an play


If you click the YES button your task is finished and your son will get a certain amount of points, which are essential in order to play with the game. It is in the kids section of the app and using those points there is the possibility to unlock accessories, animal's relatives and customize Dewo.
